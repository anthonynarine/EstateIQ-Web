## Product Page

### Goal

Explain EstateIQ as a complete financial operating system.

### Page Promise

EstateIQ connects portfolio structure, lease obligations, expenses, documents, reports, and AI insights in one system.

### Recommended Sections

1. Product overview.
2. Portfolio structure.
3. Lease ledger.
4. Expenses.
5. Documents and intake.
6. Reports.
7. AI Copilot.
8. Trust architecture.
9. CTA.
