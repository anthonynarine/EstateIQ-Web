# 01 Product Overview

## Canonical Positioning

EstateIQ is the Financial Operating System for Small Landlords.

EstateIQ helps independent landlords understand what their portfolio earns, spends, and owes through deterministic financial records, document intelligence, and AI-powered insights.

## What EstateIQ Is

EstateIQ is a finance-first operating system for small real estate portfolio owners. It is built around structured portfolio data, lease-scoped receivables, owner-side expenses, documents, reporting, and an AI Copilot that explains verified data.

The product is designed for owners and operators with roughly 1-50 units who need clearer financial control without enterprise property management complexity.

## What EstateIQ Is Not

EstateIQ is not a generic rent tracker.

EstateIQ is not just a property directory.

EstateIQ is not built around tenant self-service as the primary product promise.

EstateIQ is not an AI chatbot that guesses from loosely connected data.

EstateIQ is not a replacement for accounting, tax, legal, or professional financial advice.

## Product Thesis

Create trusted financial truth first. Let AI interpret that truth second.

The main app consistently reinforces this operating model:

- Organization-scoped data safety is the tenancy boundary.
- Buildings and units define portfolio structure.
- Leases define occupancy and rent obligations.
- Charges, payments, and allocations form the receivables ledger.
- Expenses capture owner-side spend.
- Documents provide supporting evidence.
- Reports synthesize deterministic financial facts.
- AI explains selector-backed facts and fails closed when it lacks coverage.

## Current Product

Current implemented product surfaces include:

- Authenticated dashboard route tree.
- Organization-scoped portfolio workspace.
- Buildings and units.
- Tenants and lease workflows.
- Lease-driven occupancy.
- Billing ledger with charges, payments, allocations, lease ledger page, rent posting, payment recording, and delinquency.
- Revenue workspace reading from billing ledger truth.
- Expenses with vendors, categories, scopes, archive behavior, and reporting.
- Document vault / records workspace with private file handling and metadata.
- Document inbox / intake flow for forwarded documents and reviewable expense drafts.
- Tax documents workspace with deterministic readiness scoring.
- Building Financials backend domain for mortgages, property tax profiles, insurance profiles, recurring obligations, and building snapshots.
- Reports dashboard with cash flow, occupancy, attention summaries, and building rollups.
- AI Copilot with Product Guide mode and selector-backed Portfolio Analyst mode.
- Subscription plans and Stripe-hosted checkout/customer portal.
