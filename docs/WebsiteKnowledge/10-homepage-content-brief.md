# 10 Homepage Content Brief

## Homepage Goal

The homepage should quickly establish EstateIQ as a serious financial operating system for independent landlords.

It should answer:

- What is EstateIQ?
- Who is it for?
- What financial problem does it solve?
- Why is it different from property management software?
- Why can its AI be trusted?
- What should I do next?

## Primary Hero

Headline:

EstateIQ

Supporting headline:

The Financial Operating System for Small Landlords.

Subcopy:

EstateIQ helps independent landlords understand what their portfolio earns, spends, and owes through ledger-backed records, document intelligence, and AI-powered insights.

Primary CTA:

Get Started

Secondary CTA:

See Product

## Narrative Order

### 1. Hero

Purpose:

Establish brand, category, audience, and core outcome.

Do not overload with feature lists.

### 2. Problem

Message:

Small landlords run financial businesses with records scattered across spreadsheets, bank apps, receipts, leases, and inboxes.

Proof points:

- Cash flow is hard to reconcile.
- Open balances are easy to lose track of.
- Expenses and receipts are disconnected.
- Tax readiness arrives too late.
- AI is risky when it is not grounded in records.

### 3. Solution

Message:

EstateIQ creates a structured financial operating layer around the portfolio.

Pillars:

- Portfolio structure.
- Lease ledger.
- Expenses and documents.
- Reports and tax readiness.
- Grounded AI Copilot.

### 4. Product System

Message:

EstateIQ connects the records that determine financial truth.

Suggested visual:

Organization -> Buildings/Units -> Leases -> Ledger -> Reports -> Copilot.

### 5. Ledger Differentiator

Message:

Balances come from charges, payments, and allocations, not manually edited totals.

This should be one of the homepage's strongest proof sections.

### 6. Documents and Evidence

Message:

Keep bills, receipts, statements, and lease documents connected to the records they support.

Be clear that confirmation is required before documents become financial records.

### 7. Grounded AI

Message:

Copilot explains verified data. It does not invent financial truth.

Mention source-backed answers, confidence metadata, and fail-closed behavior only if the UI will support the claim visually.

### 8. Trust

Message:

EstateIQ is built around organization-scoped data, private files, Stripe-hosted billing, and deterministic financial logic.

Avoid compliance claims.

### 9. CTA

Message:

Move from scattered records to a clear financial operating system.

## Copy Constraints

Do not use fake metrics.

Do not use fake customer quotes.

Do not imply production features that are roadmap-only.

Do not describe EstateIQ as a tenant portal or rent collection product.

Do not make AI the first or only product story.

## Homepage Tone

Modern, calm, financially serious, and concise.

The page should feel like a premium SaaS command center, not a playful landlord app.
