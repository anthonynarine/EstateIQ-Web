## Recommended Public Pages

### Home

Purpose:

Introduce EstateIQ, the category, the core outcome, and the main product pillars.

Primary message:

EstateIQ helps independent landlords understand what their portfolio earns, spends, and owes.

### Product

Purpose:

Explain the whole financial operating system model.

Recommended sections:

- Portfolio structure.
- Lease and ledger truth.
- Expenses and documents.
- Reports and tax readiness.
- AI Copilot.

### Features

Purpose:

Catalog current capabilities without generic feature bloat.

Recommended grouping:

- Portfolio setup.
- Ledger and revenue.
- Expenses.
- Documents and intake.
- Reports.
- Tax readiness.
- Building Financials.
- Subscriptions and plan limits if public.

### AI Copilot

Purpose:

Explain grounded AI and how it differs from a generic chatbot.

Must include:

- AI reads verified records.
- Deterministic tools provide facts.
- LLM presents, not invents.
- Fails closed when unsupported.

### Ledger

Purpose:

Make ledger-first accounting a product differentiator.

Must include:

- Charges.
- Payments.
- Allocations.
- Derived balances.
- Delinquency.
- Revenue/reporting connection.

### Documents / Records

Purpose:

Explain the evidence layer and intake workflow.

Must include:

- Private document vault.
- Records connected to financial context.
- Intake email/document inbox.
- User confirmation before expense creation.

### Pricing

Purpose:

Explain plans and limits once pricing is confirmed.

Important:

Pricing needs human confirmation. Source docs contain conflicting public hypotheses and implemented plan values.

### Docs

Purpose:

Public product docs, guides, or help center.

Do not expose internal architecture docs verbatim unless intentionally curated.

### Blog

Purpose:

Education for small landlords around financial clarity, tax readiness, portfolio operations, and grounded AI.

Avoid generic SEO filler.

### About

Purpose:

Explain the mission and why EstateIQ exists.

Tone:

Serious, founder-led, product-disciplined.
