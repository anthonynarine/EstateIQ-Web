# Brand Voice

## Voice Attributes

EstateIQ should sound confident, intelligent, calm, trustworthy, modern, and concise.

The writing should feel like a serious product built by people who understand financial operations. It should never feel like a speculative AI demo, a real estate guru brand, or generic SaaS copy.

## Style

Use plain language. Explain the financial benefit directly.

Write with authority, but avoid overclaiming. If a statement depends on product functionality, make sure the product can support it.

Prefer specific nouns over broad adjectives. "Property-level cash flow" is stronger than "powerful insights." "Document-backed answers" is stronger than "AI magic."

Keep sentences controlled. Use short paragraphs. Let the product sound precise rather than loud.

## What EstateIQ Should Sound Like

EstateIQ can sound similar in quality and restraint to Stripe, Ramp, Linear, OpenAI, and Mercury: modern, clear, and confident.

The goal is not to copy their phrasing. The goal is to share their discipline: concise language, clear hierarchy, useful specificity, and minimal exaggeration.

## Copy Rules

Lead with the customer problem or outcome.

Use "EstateIQ" as the product name. Use "Financial Operating System" as positioning.

Describe AI as grounded in records, documents, and financial context.

Use active voice.

Prefer "independent landlords" and "small real estate portfolios" over vague audience terms.

Avoid cleverness when clarity is more useful.

## Avoid

Avoid hype, buzzwords, exaggerated AI claims, and marketing filler.

Do not use phrases like:

- Revolutionary
- Game-changing
- Unlock your potential
- Supercharge everything
- AI magic
- The future of real estate
- Effortless wealth
- Guaranteed savings
- Set it and forget it

Avoid suggesting that EstateIQ replaces professional judgment, accounting advice, tax advice, legal advice, or lender requirements.

## Preferred Tone Examples

Use: "Understand what each property earns, spends, and owes."

Avoid: "Unlock unprecedented portfolio intelligence with next-gen AI."

Use: "Connect records, documents, and AI-powered insights in one financial operating layer."

Avoid: "Manage your empire with a revolutionary AI copilot."

Use: "See cash flow, debt, and obligations before they become surprises."

Avoid: "Never worry about your rentals again."
