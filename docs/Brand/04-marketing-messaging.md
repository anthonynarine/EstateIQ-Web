# Marketing Messaging

## Primary Headline

EstateIQ

The Financial Operating System for Small Landlords.

## Primary Value Propositions

Understand what your portfolio earns, spends, and owes.

Connect transactions, documents, and property-level financial records in one operating layer.

Use AI-powered insights grounded in deterministic records and source documents.

See cash flow, debt, obligations, and risk before they become surprises.

Run a small real estate portfolio with the clarity of a finance team.

## Elevator Pitch

EstateIQ is an AI-native Financial Operating System for independent landlords. It connects financial records, documents, and portfolio context so owners can understand performance, obligations, and risk across every property.

## Short Product Description

EstateIQ helps independent landlords understand what their portfolio earns, spends, and owes through deterministic financial records, document intelligence, and AI-powered insights.

## Long Product Description

EstateIQ is built for independent landlords and small real estate portfolios that need financial clarity without enterprise overhead. It brings together transactions, property-level records, documents, debt, and obligations into a single operating layer.

Instead of acting like traditional property management software, EstateIQ focuses on financial truth: what changed, what is owed, which properties are healthy, which records support the answer, and where the owner should pay attention next.

AI in EstateIQ is grounded in the portfolio's records and documents, helping owners ask better questions, identify patterns, and make faster decisions without losing traceability.

## Differentiators

Built specifically for independent landlords and small portfolios.

Financial operating system, not generic property management software.

Deterministic records first, AI-powered insights second.

Property-level visibility across earnings, expenses, debt, documents, and obligations.

Calm command-center interface designed for financial decisions.

Document intelligence connected to portfolio context.

## Messaging Hierarchy

Start with the product name and positioning. EstateIQ should be visible before the category explanation.

Then explain the core customer outcome: understanding what the portfolio earns, spends, and owes.

Then explain the system: deterministic records, document intelligence, and AI-powered insights.

Then differentiate from traditional property management software.

Then support with use cases, product proof, trust signals, pricing, and calls to action.
